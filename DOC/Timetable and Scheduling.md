# Timetable and Scheduling

## Fixed Daily Schedule (All Departments — Same Timetable)

| Slot | Time | Type | Counts as Period? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1st Hour | 7:30 AM – 10:00 AM | Lab | ✅ Yes |
| 2nd Hour | 10:30 AM – 11:30 AM | Theory | ✅ Yes |
| 3rd Hour | 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM | Theory | ✅ Yes |
| **Lunch** | **12:30 PM – 1:30 PM** | **Break** | ❌ Not a period |
| 4th Hour | 1:30 PM – 2:30 PM | Theory | ✅ Yes |
| 5th Hour | 2:45 PM – 5:15 PM | Lab | ✅ Yes |

> **5 attendance periods per day.** Lunch is never an attendance slot.

## Batch Structure (Unified Terminology)

The word **"Batch"** is used uniformly throughout the system — for both theory and lab groupings.

| Class Type | Batches | Students per Batch |
|---|---|---|
| Theory (Hours 2, 3, 4) | 2 Batches (A, B) | ~50 each |
| Lab / Practical (Hours 1, 5) | 4 Batches (1, 2, 3, 4) | ~25 each |

- For **100 students in a year**: Theory → Batch A (50) + Batch B (50). Lab → Batch 1, 2, 3, 4 (25 each).
- The [[Year Co-ordinator]] proposes the batch groupings; the [[Principal login|Principal]] approves.
- For lab sessions: **1 dedicated staff per batch** — each logs in independently.

## Saturday Working Days
- Saturdays are **off by default**.
- The [[Principal login|Principal]] explicitly enables a Saturday before that date.
- Enabled Saturday = normal working day (5 periods counted).
- Non-enabled Saturday = College Holiday (excluded from denominator entirely).

## College Holidays
- Holidays must be declared by the Principal **before the day begins**.
- Once declared, the system **locks out all attendance submissions** for that day.
- No retroactive holiday entry is allowed.

## Field Practice Attendance
- Taken by the **Subject Staff overseeing that batch**.
- Same 20-minute submission window applies.
