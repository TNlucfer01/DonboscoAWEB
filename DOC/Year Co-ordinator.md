# Year Co-ordinator
**1 Year Co-ordinator per year.** Responsible for all students in that year — both theory batches and all lab batches, across all semesters.

## Home Screen (Dashboard)
The YC lands on a dashboard with:
- **Attendance summary graphs**: overall year attendance %, per-batch %, trend over time.
- **Key stats**: total students, number below 80%, pending OD/IL entries.
- **Quick actions**: Add Student, Enter OD/Leave, View Attendance.

## Responsibilities

### 1. Adding Students
- YC fills in student details (name, roll number, parent phone, etc.) **+ the batch number** to assign the student directly.
- Students are added one at a time or bulk uploaded.

### 2. Batch Management
- Propose batch groupings (2 theory batches + 4 lab batches) for approval by the Principal.

### 3. Subject Mapping
- Map globally created subjects to the correct year:
  - **Regular subjects**: all students in that year auto-enrolled.
  - **Elective** (3rd year): YC manually selects specific students.
  - **ELM** (4th year): YC manually maps all 4th year students.

### 4. OD / Informed Leave Entry
- YC can pre-enter OD and Informed Leave **for future days only**.
- OD entry requires a mandatory reason text field.
- Informed Leave requires a signed physical form (YC + Principal checks % ≥ 80% before signing).
- Pre-entered entries lock the student's row in the staff's attendance table.

### 5. View Attendance (Multiple Views)
The YC can view attendance for their year in several ways:

| View Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| **By Batch** | Select a batch → See all students and their attendance summary |
| **By Subject** | Select a subject → See total hours and attendance for that subject |
| **By Calendar + Period** | Select a date from a calendar → Select period → See who was present/absent |
| **Attendance %** | See each student's overall attendance percentage |

> The YC can see attendance **for their year only**.

### 6. Reports
- Generate and export attendance reports for their year (PDF/Excel).
