# User Account Management

## Overview
3 login roles exist: **Principal**, **Year Co-ordinator**, and **Subject Staff**.

## Principal Account (System Setup)
- The Principal's account is **pre-seeded at system deployment** by the developer/IT team.
- There is no self-registration for the Principal role.
- Default credentials are provided at installation; the Principal should change the password on first login.

## Account Creation (by Principal)
- The Principal creates accounts for Year Co-ordinators and Subject Staff via the admin panel.
- Each account requires: Name, Phone Number (for OTP reset), Role, Department.
- A default password is issued on creation.
- If a new staff joins mid-semester, Principal creates their account and assigns them immediately.
- If a staff is removed, Principal reassigns their subjects/batches to another staff.

## Password Reset (Self-Service OTP Flow)
Any user who forgets their password:
1. Clicks **"Forgot Password"** on the login screen.
2. Enters their registered phone number.
3. Receives a **one-time OTP** via SMS.
4. Enters the OTP and sets a new password.
5. Login restored — no admin intervention needed.

## Subject Staff Assignment
- Staff are assigned to specific subjects and batch/section slots by the Principal.
- A staff member only sees attendance screens for their assigned subjects/batches.
